What is N'STANT?
N'Stant is simply way to decide on what to eat base on the time and place.
It helps by having a list of suggesstions near or far from your location.
Effortlessly navigate through the options and choose from the following.
Users use either their location or set a location.
The user is presented with the map showing all restaurants around his location or location set.
From there he can choose a restaurant based on the price, the rating and the address.
